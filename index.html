Featured Project

Energy Market Stress Test: Heavy Crude, Carbon Risk & Supply Dynamics

A multi-phase analysis exploring how heavy crude expansion, refining complexity, carbon pricing, and geopolitical disruptions affect oil prices, margins, and market resilience.

  • Modeled oil price response to supply shocks
  • Analyzed heavy crude refining complexity
  • Simulated carbon cost impact on margins
  • Stress-tested profitability under different market scenarios
  • Extended the model to Strait of Hormuz disruption scenarios

Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Tableau, Excel

Phase 1: Margin Sensitivity Heatmap

How carbon price and heavy crude discount interact to compress margins.

Phase 2: Supply Shock & Price Response

Estimated oil price response under different heavy crude expansion scenarios.

Phase 3: Refining Complexity

Why heavy crude is structurally harder and costlier to refine.

Phase 4–5: Carbon + Supply Stress Test

When heavy crude expansion stops making economic sense.

Phase 6: Strait of Hormuz Disruption

Stress test of chokepoint disruption and global oil price sensitivity.
